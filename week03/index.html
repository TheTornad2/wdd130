Yaque River Rafting

Our mission is to provide exhilarating rafting experiences while preserving the natural beauty of the Yaque River. We believe in adventure, safety, and environmental stewardship.

History

Founded in 2005, Yaque River Rafting began with a small team of passionate river guides. Over the years, we've grown to become the premier rafting company in the region, serving thousands of adventure seekers while maintaining our commitment to safety and sustainability.
